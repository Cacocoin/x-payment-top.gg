What is X-Payment? X-Payment is your Social P2P and merchant solution for Discord.
What is X-Payment good at? As an off-chain layer of the X-Cash blockchain, X-Payment can send Public & Private transactions to the people you want to send to instantly, for free.
How does X-Payment work? X-Payment is designed to sit on top of any Social Media platform so you can use it where ever you call home.
What is X-Payment's mission? X-Payment's mission is to give you the most convenient cryptocurrency experience.
Which platforms is X-Payment on? X-Payment is currently on Discord and Twitter, and we plan to roll out onto new platforms.
What can X-Payment do? Many things!
X-Payment started as a Discord tipping bot for the X-Cash community. It has since had a load of updates including; Merchant system; Integrated (simplified) receiver addresses; private transaction; its own Platform.
How popular is X-Payment? As of 21 September, X-Payment is listed on 94 Discord servers, has 5853 registered wallets, and has a 50 THOUSAND USER REACH.
Merchant The Merchant System lets Discord users purchase roles and access secret channels inside servers for as long as the role is valid.
